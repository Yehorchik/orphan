Our Project

Hello there!

This project was created with one goal in mind, to create a website where we can help raise awareness within the online community that there are kids in the world who cannot go home to parents, who feel like they don't have anyone they can really rely on. Although this project is still conceptual and not in anyway close to the scale we want it to be, during the week we had, we came up with an idea that has unlimited potential to grow and blossom into something where other people can be inspired, doing something from small to big to help out the community of kids who don't have a home to go back to.
